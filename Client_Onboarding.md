# Skill: Client Onboarding

## When to use
- A new client has agreed on working with EP
- You need to set a meeting cadence, expectations 

## Outcome (definition of done)
- The client has reviewed Proposal with the Coach and signed it
- At least the first meeting (monthly or Initiation is scheduled)
- The client set up GoCardless (BG to confirm)


## Checklist
- [ ] Initial onboarding email sent
- [ ] Proposal signed by client and located in EP Dropbox
- [ ] Schedule at least first meeting
- [ ] Introduce Briony and Schedule 12-18 months of meetings quarterlies, monthlys, annual - Briony will help
- [ ] Briony to Send calendar invites
- [ ] Confirm attendee list and roles
- [ ] Set up Metronome
- [ ] Ask Brad Giles to set up Teachable
